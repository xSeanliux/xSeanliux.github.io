I'm Sean, nice to meet you! I graduated in 2021 with a dual degree in Linguistics and Computer Science from the [University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign](https://illinois.edu/). I'm a retired competitive programmer, theoryhead, and am interested in ancient languages. I'm always trying new things -- I used to speedcube (here's my [WCA profile](https://www.worldcubeassociation.org/persons/2014LIUZ08)); now I'm trying to learn a style of dance known as popping. 

I'm currently working with Professor [Tandy Warnow](https://tandy.cs.illinois.edu/) in the [CPHL group](https://tandy.cs.illinois.edu/histling.html) to develop better algorithms to infer language family trees. In Summer 2024, I was busy improving the markets as a software enginner intern at [Optiver Δ](https://optiver.com/), and am now there as a full-time software engineer.

I'm a bilingual native speaker in both Mandarin Chinese and English, and can hold a conversation in Japanese; I've also taken a semester of [Hittite](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hittites) from the wonderful [Prof. Shosted](https://linguistics.illinois.edu/directory/profile/rshosted) at UIUC.
